Privacy Policy

Emoji Supercharge

This page explains what data the Emoji Supercharge tools access and how that data is handled.

Hub Terms Support

What the Service Accesses

Depending on the feature, the service may access the active Google Slides presentation, selected slide elements, and Drive files or file metadata needed to replace or generate slide content.

What the Service Stores

The service may store limited operational data such as user preferences, last-used image identifiers, or task state needed to support expected product behavior.

What the Service Does Not Do

  • It is not intended to sell personal data.
  • It is not intended to use customer data for advertising.
  • It does not request broader Google account access than necessary for the stated features.

Retention and Access

Access to service data and configuration is limited to authorized users and maintainers. Data may be removed, reset, or rotated as part of normal maintenance.
